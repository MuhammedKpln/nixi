Nixy is a simple and elegant note taking app that syncs with your Nextcloud account. You can create, edit and delete notes in markdown format, and organize them with tags and folders. Nixy lets you access your notes offline, and supports dark mode, search, and rich text preview. Whether you need to jot down a quick reminder, write a long article, or keep track of your tasks, Nixy is the perfect companion for your Nextcloud notes.
